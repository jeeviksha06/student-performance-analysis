# Student Performance Analysis - Insights

## Dataset Overview
- Total Students Analyzed: 30
- Columns: Student Name, Math, Science, English Scores, Attendance, Grade

## Key Findings

### 1. Subject-wise Performance
- Average Math Score: 77.37
- Average Science Score: 78.10 (highest among all subjects)
- Average English Score: 76.63 (lowest among all subjects)
- Science is the strongest subject overall

### 2. Grade Distribution
- A Grade: 43.3% — Nearly half the class is excelling
- B Grade: 30.0% — Good performance
- C Grade: 20.0% — Average performance
- D Grade: 6.7% — Needs improvement

### 3. Attendance
- Average Attendance: 86.87%
- Most students have good attendance

### 4. Top Performer
- Aaron scored highest in Math (96)
- High scorers generally have 95%+ attendance

## Conclusions
1. Science scores are slightly higher than Math and English
2. Majority of students (73.3%) are performing well (A or B grade)
3. Students with higher attendance tend to score better
4. English needs more focus as it has the lowest average score
